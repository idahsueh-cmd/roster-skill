# Roster — Join

A new collaborator is joining an ongoing session. Your job is to brief them and add them to the shared record.

**Step 1 — Read `ROSTER.md`** (and `ROSTER-detail.md` if it exists). Do not ask anything yet.

**Step 2 — Brief the new person in plain language:**
- What this session is about (the overall goal)
- Who is working on what
- What areas are off-limits (other people's session ownership zones — do not touch without going through the conflict flow)
- What files need group agreement before anyone touches them

**Step 3 — Ask in a single message:**
1. New person's name?
2. What are they building today? (one sentence)

**Step 4 — After they answer:**
- Map their task to the files/directories they'll likely touch
- Assign Session Owner + Consult / Notify for their new areas
- Update the `ROSTER.md` Ownership table

**Step 5 — Tell the new person:**
"You're in. If you need to touch someone else's area, paste the conflict prompt and resolve it first. Check current state anytime with the status prompt."
